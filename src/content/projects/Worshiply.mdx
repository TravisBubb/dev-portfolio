A simple, focused SaaS for small churches.

Worshiply is a lightweight church management platform designed for smaller churches that don't need (or want) bloated enterprise tools. It prioritizes simplicity, ease of use, and affordability. I'm currently building the MVP with a focus on member management and contributionw tracking.

## Features

- Native & Google Authentication
- Member Tracking & Management

## Coming Soon

- Contribution Tracking Across Multiple Configurable "Buckets"
- Contribution Reporting
- Church Directory Generation

## Tech Stack 

- Go
- Next.js & Typescript
- Tailwindcss
- PostgreSQL
